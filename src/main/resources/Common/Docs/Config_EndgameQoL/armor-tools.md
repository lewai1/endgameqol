## <#55ffff>Armor Sets</#55ffff>

All endgame armor provides Physical and Projectile damage resistance plus stat bonuses. Higher tiers gain passive mana regeneration and HP regeneration.

### <#c0c0c0>Mithril Armor</#c0c0c0> (4 pieces)

**Rarity** — Epic (ItemLevel 50)

**Resistance** — 18% Physical per piece (~50% full set)

**Stats** — +30 Health, +19 Mana (chest), 6% Signature enhancement

**Mana Regen** — +0.5/sec per piece (+2.0/sec full set)

**Durability** — 220

**Recipe (chest)** — 24 Mithril Bars, 8 Storm Leather, 80 Void Essence at Armor Bench Tier 3

### <#9955dd>Onyxium Armor</#9955dd> (4 pieces)

**Resistance** — ~61% Physical full set. Gains Fire resistance.

**Mana Regen** — +0.75/sec per piece (+3.0/sec full set)

**HP Regen** — +0.5 HP/sec per piece (+2.0/sec full set) after 15 seconds without taking damage

### <gradient data="#ff5555:#55ffff:#d16eff">Prisma Armor</gradient> (4 pieces)

**Rarity** — Legendary (ItemLevel 55)

**Resistance** — 28% Physical per piece (~78% full set), 15% Fire and Fall per piece (~45% full set)

**Stats** — +47 Health, +30 Mana (chest), 10% Signature enhancement

**Mana Regen** — +1.0/sec per piece (+4.0/sec full set)

**HP Regen** — +0.75 HP/sec per piece (+3.0/sec full set) after 15 seconds without taking damage

**Durability** — 400

**Recipe (chest)** — 8 Prisma Bars, 5 Prismic Leather, 1 Emerald Gem at Endgame Bench Tier 5

! Mana regen is always active, even during combat. HP regen pauses when taking damage and resumes after 15 seconds. Both are configurable per-tier in <#55ffff>/egconfig</#55ffff>.

## <#ff6600>Tools</#ff6600>

### Pickaxes

**<#55ffff>Mithril Pickaxe</#55ffff>** — 650 durability. Full power on Copper through Gold ores, half power on Thorium/Cobalt, quarter power on Mithril.

**<#ff6600>Onyxium Pickaxe</#ff6600>** — Improved mining speed over Mithril.

**<#d16eff>Prisma Pickaxe</#d16eff>** — Legendary (ItemLevel 66). 1500 durability (0.1 loss per block). Full power on most ores (including Thorium, Cobalt, Adamantite), 75% on Mithril. ~6x better effective durability than Mithril. Press <#ff8800>Ability3</#ff8800> to toggle <#d16eff>3x3 Area Break</#d16eff> mode — breaking a block also breaks surrounding blocks in a 3x3 area (rocks and ores only). Orange glow when active.

### Shovels
**Iron Shovel**, **Cobalt Shovel**, **Thorium Shovel** — Progressive dirt/soil digging efficiency.

### Hatchets

**<#ff6600>Onyxium Hatchet</#ff6600>** — High-tier wood chopping tool.

**<#d16eff>Prisma Hatchet</#d16eff>** — Legendary (ItemLevel 66). 1500 durability. Best-in-class wood chopping with prismatic glow. Press <#ff8800>Ability3</#ff8800> to toggle <#d16eff>3x3 Area Break</#d16eff> mode — breaking a block also breaks surrounding blocks in a 3x3 area (wood and soft blocks only). Orange glow when active. 1-second cooldown between toggles. Crafted at Workbench.

## <#55ffff>Deployables</#55ffff>

### Mana Totem

A deployable support totem that regenerates mana for nearby allies.

**Effect** — 6 mana/sec for players within range

**Duration** — 12 seconds

**Cooldown** — 12 seconds (shared deployable cooldown)

**Visual** — Blue azure spiral particles

**Recipe** — 10 Life Essence, 5 Blue Crystal, 5 Thorium Bars at Endgame Bench Tier 2

Throw to deploy at target location. Works like the Healing Totem but for mana. Great for staff users and extended boss fights.

## <#55ff55>Consumables</#55ff55>

### Mana Potions

6 variants in two categories:

**Instant restore** — Small, Standard (10 Mana), Large. Only usable when Mana is below 100%. Stack of 10.

**Regen over time** — Small Regen, Standard Regen, Large Regen. Gradual mana recovery.

**Recipe (Standard)** — 1 Empty Potion, 10 Life Essence, 5 Crystal Blue at Alchemy Bench.

### Food

**Alpha Trex Meat (Raw)** — Dropped by Alpha Rex.

**Alpha Trex Meat (Cooked)** — Cook raw meat at a Campfire (6s). Applies instant healing. Stacks to 25.

## <#d16eff>Gliders</#d16eff>

**Standard Glider** — Basic glider for slow descent.

**Advanced Glider** — Improved version.

**<#d16eff>Void-Powered Glider</#d16eff>** — Legendary. Very slow fall (20x multiplier), 110% forward speed, terminal velocity -3. Secondary boosts 45 force upward (costs 5 Void Essence per use). Eliminates stamina consumption while gliding. Recipe: 1 Onyxium Bar, 2 Prismic Leather, 10 Linen Fabric, 6 Sticks at Endgame Bench Tier 4.

## <#aaaaaa>Backpack Upgrades</#aaaaaa>

3 tiers of one-time-use backpack expansion items:

**<#aaaaaa>Endgame Backpack Upgrade I</#aaaaaa>** — +9 slots. 1 Voidheart, 8 Adamantite Bars, 8 Apex Sovereign Leather at Endgame Bench Tier 3. Requires Memories Level 5.

**<#55ffff>Endgame Backpack Upgrade II</#55ffff>** — +9 slots. 2 Voidheart, 4 Mithril Bars, 10 Apex Sovereign Leather at Endgame Bench Tier 4. Requires Memories Level 5.

**<#ff6600>Endgame Backpack Upgrade III</#ff6600>** — +9 slots. 4 Voidheart, 6 Onyxium Bars, 12 Apex Sovereign Leather at Endgame Bench Tier 5. Requires Memories Level 5.

Each upgrade can only be used once per character.

## <#d16eff>Portal Keys & Shards</#d16eff>

Consumable items that open dungeon and boss encounter portals.

**<#55ffff>Frozen Dungeon Key</#55ffff>** — Opens the Frozen Dungeon instance (20 min). Recipe: 10 Adamantite Bars, 5 Mithril Bars, 30 Fire Essence at Endgame Bench Tier 2.

**<#55ff55>Swamp Dungeon Key</#55ff55>** — Opens the Swamp Dungeon instance (90 min). Recipe: 10 Adamantite Bars, 5 Mithril Bars, 30 Fire Essence at Endgame Bench Tier 3.

**<#d16eff>Shard of the Void</#d16eff>** — Opens the Void Realm (30 min). Recipe: 5 Onyxium Bars, 50 Void Essence at Endgame Bench Tier 4.

**Shard of the Radiant Light** — Access to Luminia realm (WIP).

**Ancient Gateway** — Dungeon entrance portal.
