# <gradient data="#55ffff:#aaaaaa:#55ffff">Frozen Dungeon</gradient>

A multi-level ice cavern built by <#55ffff>ReyZ41</#55ffff>, the Frozen Dungeon is the first major dungeon in Endgame & QoL. Fight through frost-themed enemies, collect <#55ffff>Flocons</#55ffff>, trade with hidden merchants, loot chests, and face the <#55ffff>Dragon Frost</#55ffff> boss at the end.

---

## <#55ffff>Entering the Dungeon</#55ffff>

Craft a <#55ffff>Frozen Dungeon Key</#55ffff> at the Endgame Bench (Tier 2) using 10 Adamantite Bars, 5 Mithril Bars, and 30 Fire Essence. Use it on an <#aaaaaa>Ancient Portal</#aaaaaa> to open the Frozen Dungeon instance (20 min time limit). The dungeon loads as a separate instance, so your main world progress is unaffected.

---

## <#55ffff>Dungeon Currency — Flocons</#55ffff>

Every enemy in the dungeon drops <#55ffff>Flocons</#55ffff>, the dungeon's dedicated currency. Use them to buy gear from the hidden traders inside.

---

## <#55ffff>Enemies</#55ffff>

| Enemy | HP | Damage |
|:------|---:|:-------|
| <#55ffff>Ice Rat</#55ffff> | 35 | 18+5 Ice |
| <#55ffff>Frost Toad</#55ffff> | 124 | 18 + 35 Physical |
| <#55ffff>Frost Feran</#55ffff> | 150 | 35 Physical |
| <#55ffff>Frost Golem</#55ffff> | 300 | 28+12 Ice |
| <#55ffff>Frost Yeti</#55ffff> | 400 | 40+15 Ice |
| <#55ffff>Spirit Frost</#55ffff> | 200 | — |

**<#55ffff>Ice Rat</#55ffff>** — Drops Light Hides and 1-2 Flocons.

**<#55ffff>Frost Toad</#55ffff>** — Tongue grab pulls you in, then headbutts. 10m range. Drops Medium Hides, Ice Essence, 1-3 Flocons.

**<#55ffff>Frost Feran</#55ffff>** — Humanoid warrior wielding a Bone Frost Sword. Drops Medium Hides, Cobalt Bars, 2-3 Flocons.

**<#55ffff>Frost Golem</#55ffff>** — Slow elemental golem. Drops Ice Essence, Sapphires, Cobalt Bars, 2-4 Flocons.

**<#55ffff>Frost Yeti</#55ffff>** — Tough mountain creature. Drops Storm Hides, Ice Essence, Mithril Bars, 3-5 Flocons.

**<#55ffff>Spirit Frost</#55ffff>** — Spectral frost entity. Summoned by Dragon Frost in Phase 3 (up to 4 at once).

! Skeletons (Soldiers, Archers, Mages, Rangers, Knights, Archmages) are scattered throughout the dungeon.

---

## <#55ffff>Traders</#55ffff>

**<#55ffff>Korvyn</#55ffff>** — Hidden trader inside the dungeon. Sells Mithril gear, the <#55ffff>Frostbite Blade</#55ffff> (45 Flocons), and potions. Stock restocks daily and resets on dungeon re-entry. Unique items have limited stock (3), potions are plentiful (99).

**<#55ffff>Vorthak</#55ffff>** — Found in the Forgotten Temple area. Sells consumables for Adamantite/Mithril Bars. See the Crafting page for full trade details.

---

## <#55ffff>Chest Loot</#55ffff>

The dungeon has 6 progressive loot zones with randomized chest contents:

**Rat Zone** — Basic supplies and early materials

**Toad Zone** — Mid-tier materials and Flocons

**Feran Zone** — Cobalt-tier gear and crafting materials

**Golem Zone** — Higher-tier materials and rare drops

**Yeti Zone** — Mithril-tier materials and endgame ingredients

**Dragon Zone** — Best loot in the dungeon, endgame gear

---

## <#55ffff>Boss — Dragon Frost</#55ffff>

The Dragon Frost awaits at the deepest level. See the Bosses page for full details on the 3-phase fight.

---

## <#aaaaaa>Weather & Atmosphere</#aaaaaa>

The dungeon features a light snow environment with icy blue fog. Visibility has been improved in v4.0.0 (reduced from Snow_Heavy to Snow_Light with lower fog density).

---

## <#aaaaaa>Block Protection</#aaaaaa>

Block protection is <#55ff55>enabled by default</#55ff55> in v4.0.0. Blocks inside the Frozen Dungeon are unbreakable and unplaceable, except for <#55ffff>Mithril Snow Ore</#55ffff> which can still be mined. Disable in <#55ffff>/egconfig</#55ffff> under the Misc tab if desired.
