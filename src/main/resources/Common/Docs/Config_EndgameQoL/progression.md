# <gradient data="#aaaaaa:#55ffff:#ff6600:#d16eff">Progression</gradient>

Endgame & QoL extends Hytale's crafting progression with four material tiers beyond vanilla.

---

## <#aaaaaa>Tier 1 — Adamantite</#aaaaaa>

The bridge between vanilla and endgame content. Adamantite weapons and armor are crafted at the standard Weapon Bench and Armor Bench (Tier 3).

**Key items** — Adamantite Longsword, Spear, Staff, and full armor set.

**Warden's Trial** — Tier I challenge is aimed at this gear level.

**Instance** — <#55ffff>Frozen Dungeon</#55ffff> (Dragon Frost boss). Access via <#55ffff>Frozen Dungeon Key</#55ffff>.

---

## <#55ffff>Tier 2 — Mithril</#55ffff>

The first true endgame tier. Mithril Bars are found in Frozen Dungeon chests, mined from Mithril Snow Ore inside the Frozen Dungeon, or traded from Vorthak. Requires the <#55ffff>Endgame Workbench</#55ffff> at Tier 3.

**Key items** — Full weapon set (sword, daggers, longsword, spear, battleaxe, staff, shortbow, shield), full armor set, Mithril Pickaxe.

**Stat bonuses** — Mithril weapons grant Signature Energy and Stamina bonuses. Mithril armor provides +30 Health, +19 Mana per chest piece.

**Mana Regen** — +0.5/sec per armor piece (+2.0/sec full set). Always active, even in combat.

**Backstab damage** — All Mithril daggers deal 1.5x backstab damage when hitting from behind.

**Instance** — <#55ff55>Swamp Dungeon</#55ff55> (Hedera boss). Access via <#55ff55>Swamp Dungeon Key</#55ff55>.

**Materials** — <#aaaaaa>Mithril Bars</#aaaaaa>, <#aaaaaa>Storm Leather</#aaaaaa>, <#aaaaaa>Voidheart</#aaaaaa>, <#aaaaaa>Void Essence</#aaaaaa>

---

## <#ff6600>Tier 3 — Onyxium</#ff6600>

A step above Mithril with stronger stats and unique effects. Onyxium Bars are found in Swamp Dungeon chests, traded from Morghul, or dropped by Hedera and Golem Void.

**Key items** — Onyxium variants of all weapon types, full armor set, Onyxium Pickaxe, Onyxium Hatchet, Onyxium Mace.

**Special weapon effects:**
- <#ff6600>Onyxium Sword/Daggers</#ff6600> — Drain 2 stamina per hit from targets
- <#ff6600>Onyxium Spear</#ff6600> — 30% slow on stab hits (1.5s)
- <#ff6600>Onyxium Staff</#ff6600> — Secondary: Void Implosion (AOE pull + 40% slow)

**Armor bonuses:**
- ~61% Physical resist (full set)
- +0.75/sec mana regen per piece (+3.0/sec full set)
- Passive HP regen: +0.5 HP/sec per piece after 15s without damage (+2.0 HP/sec full set)

**Backstab damage** — All Onyxium daggers deal 1.5x backstab damage.

---

## <#d16eff>Tier 4 — Prisma</#d16eff>

The ultimate endgame tier. Prisma gear is crafted from <#d16eff>Prisma Bars</#d16eff> and <#d16eff>Prismic Leather</#d16eff>. Requires Endgame Workbench at Tier 5.

**<#d16eff>Prisma Sword</#d16eff>** — Signature summons 2 spectral <#d16eff>Prisma Clones</#d16eff> (5s, 15s cooldown). Every hit applies <#d16eff>Prisma Resonance</#d16eff> (30% slow). Finisher applies <#d16eff>Prisma Shatter</#d16eff> (60% slow). +200 Mana, +30 Signature Energy, +25 Stamina.

**<#d16eff>Prisma Daggers</#d16eff>** — Hits apply <#d16eff>Void Mark</#d16eff> (10s). Signature teleports behind marked targets. 3x execution damage below 25% HP. **Stance Switch** — Press Ability3 to toggle between Combat Mode (teleport behind target) and Blink Mode (teleport ~12 blocks forward). 1.5x backstab damage.

**<#d16eff>Prisma Pickaxe</#d16eff>** — Best mining tool in the game (3x Mithril for rare ores). Press <#ff8800>Ability3</#ff8800> to toggle <#d16eff>3x3 Area Break</#d16eff> mode — breaks surrounding blocks in a 3x3 area (rocks and ores only). Orange glow when active. 1500 durability.

**<#d16eff>Prisma Hatchet</#d16eff>** — Best hatchet in the game. Press <#ff8800>Ability3</#ff8800> to toggle <#d16eff>3x3 Area Break</#d16eff> mode — chops surrounding blocks in a 3x3 area (wood and soft blocks). Orange glow when active. 1500 durability.

**<#d16eff>Prisma Armor</#d16eff>** — ~78% Physical resist, 45% Fire/Fall resist. +1.0/sec mana regen per piece (+4.0/sec full set). HP regen: +0.75 HP/sec per piece after 15s without damage (+3.0 HP/sec full set).

**Instance** — <#d16eff>Void Realm</#d16eff> (Golem Void boss). Access via <#d16eff>Shard of the Void</#d16eff>.

---

## <#ff6600>Material Sources</#ff6600>

**<#55ffff>Mithril Bars</#55ffff>** — Frozen Dungeon chests, Mithril Snow Ore, Zone 4 Sewers chests.

**<#aaaaaa>Storm Leather</#aaaaaa>** — Crafted from Storm Hides (mob drops, chests).

**<#aaaaaa>Voidheart</#aaaaaa>** — Zombie Aberrant, Spectre Void, Hedera, Shadow Knight, Necromancer, Onyxium Encounter.

**<#d16eff>Void Essence</#d16eff>** — Spectre Void, Hedera, Swamp Crocodile, Zone 4 mobs.

**<#ff6600>Onyxium Bars</#ff6600>** — Smelted from Onyxium Ore, dropped by Hedera and Golem Void.

**<#d16eff>Prisma Bars</#d16eff>** — Crafted (1 Mithril + 1 Onyxium + 1 Emerald) or dropped by Golem Void.

**<#d16eff>Prismic Leather</#d16eff>** — Crafted from Prismic Hides (Storm Leather + Heavy Leather + Shadoweave + Ice Essence).

**<#55ff55>Emerald Gems</#55ff55>** — Dropped by Golem Void.

! For detailed drop sources and quantities, see the **Materials Guide**.
