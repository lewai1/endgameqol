## <#55ffff>Mob Cards</#55ffff>

Each discovered NPC shows a detailed card with:

- **HP** — Base health
- **Damage** — Attack type and values
- **Lore** — Flavor text describing the creature
- **Notable Drops** — Key items dropped
- **Location** — Where to find it
- **Kill Stats** — Your personal kill count

---

## <#aaaaaa>Undiscovered NPCs</#aaaaaa>

NPCs you haven't encountered yet appear as silhouettes with a <#aaaaaa>location hint</#aaaaaa> — enough to point you in the right direction without spoiling stats and drops.

Kill an NPC at least once to unlock its full card.

---

## <#FFD700>Kill Milestones</#FFD700>

Each NPC category has 3 kill milestone tiers. Reaching a threshold grants an item reward from the corresponding drop table.

Milestone thresholds vary by category — bosses require fewer kills (3/10/25) while creatures need more (15/40/75).
