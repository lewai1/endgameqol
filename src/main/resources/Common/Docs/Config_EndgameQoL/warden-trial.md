# <gradient data="#ff5555:#ff6600:#d16eff">Warden's Trial</gradient>

The Warden's Trial is a wave-survival challenge where you fight through 5 waves of progressively harder enemies. Survive all waves to earn tier-appropriate loot. Each tier has a boss enemy in the final wave.

---

## <#ff5555>How It Works</#ff5555>

1. Craft a <#55ffff>Warden Challenge</#55ffff> item at the Endgame Workbench
2. Use the item — it is consumed and the trial begins
3. A <#ff5555>3-second countdown</#ff5555> starts before the first wave
4. Defeat all enemies in each wave to advance
5. <#55ff55>8-second rest</#55ff55> between waves
6. Survive all 5 waves to receive rewards

**HUD** — A beautiful overlay shows wave progress, enemy count, difficulty label, and a color-coded progress bar (green > yellow > orange > red > purple).

**Death** — If you die, remaining trial NPCs are cleaned up automatically.

---

**Tier I** — 5 Adamantite Bars, 3 Storm Hides, 1 Mithril Bar

**Tier II** — 5 Mithril Bars, 3 Storm Hides, 2 Voidheart

**Tier III** — 5 Onyxium Bars, 3 Storm Hides, 3 Voidheart

**Tier IV** — 5 Prisma Bars, 5 Storm Hides, 5 Voidheart

---

## Wave Timer

Each tier has a time limit to clear all waves. If time runs out, the trial fails and remaining NPCs despawn. The HUD shows a countdown (last 10s in red).

| Tier | Time Limit | Config Key |
|:-----|:-----------|:-----------|
| I | 4m 30s | WardenTrialTimerTier1 |
| II | 6m 00s | WardenTrialTimerTier2 |
| III | 7m 30s | WardenTrialTimerTier3 |
| IV | 9m 00s | WardenTrialTimerTier4 |

Set to 0 to disable the timer for that tier.

---

## <#ff5555>Tips</#ff5555>

- The Void Necromancer summons 6 skeletons per cast — focus it first to avoid being overwhelmed
- Shadow Knights hit for 150 damage — dodge or use crowd control
- Alpha Rex is fast and aggressive — kite with ranged weapons
- Potions and Mana Totem are allowed during trials
- Rewards drop to the ground if your inventory is full
