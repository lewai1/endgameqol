# <gradient data="#d16eff:#55ffff">Trinket Pouch & Accessories</gradient>

The <#d16eff>Trinket Pouch</#d16eff> is a 5-slot portable container for equipping passive accessories. Place it in your inventory, then open it to slot in up to 5 accessories with active effects.

**Recipe** — 6 Storm Leather, 2 Voidheart, 4 Mithril Bars at Endgame Bench Tier 2.

---

## <#55ffff>Accessories</#55ffff>

### <#55ffff>Frostwalkers</#55ffff>

Walk on water by freezing the surface beneath you. Ice melts after you move away.

**Source** — Frozen Dungeon Dragon Zone legendary chest.

---

### <#55ffff>Ocean Striders</#55ffff>

2x swim speed for faster underwater exploration. Only activates when fully submerged (feet + head).

**Source** — Rare drop from Humpback Whales (15%).

---

### <#d16eff>Void Amulet</#d16eff>

Cheat death once — prevents fatal damage, then the amulet is consumed.

**Source** — Rare drop from Golem Void (15%).

---

### <#ff6600>Blazefist</#ff6600>

AOE burn effect on melee hits. 50 Fire DPS for 3 seconds. Damage and duration are configurable in /egconfig.

**Source** — Rare drop from Fire Dragons (15%).

---

### <#55ff55>Pocket Garden</#55ff55>

+2 HP/tick regeneration when standing near farming crops. Also auto-fertilizes nearby tilled soil.

**Source** — Swamp Dungeon hidden chest.

---

### <#55ff55>Hedera Seed</#55ff55>

Chance to root enemies on hit, briefly immobilizing them.

**Source** — Rare drop from Hedera (15%).

---

!! Accessory effects deactivate when the Trinket Pouch is removed from your inventory.
