# <gradient data="#FFD700:#ff6600:#FFD700">Achievements</gradient>

Endgame & QoL includes 24 achievements across 5 categories. Track your progress, earn XP rewards, and claim item rewards for completed achievements.

**Access** — Use <#55ffff>/eg achievements</#55ffff> to view your progress.

---

## <#FFD700>Categories</#FFD700>

### Combat (5 achievements)

Kill milestones for various NPCs and combat feats.

### Boss (7 achievements)

Defeat each major boss and complete boss-related challenges.

### Bounty (4 achievements)

Complete bounties, build streaks, and reach reputation ranks.

### Discovery (4 achievements)

Discover NPCs in the Bestiary and explore all dungeon areas.

### Crafting (4 achievements)

Craft legendary weapons, armor sets, and special items.

---

## <#55ffff>Rewards</#55ffff>

Each achievement grants an <#FFD700>XP reward</#FFD700> upon completion. Some achievements also unlock <#d16eff>item rewards</#d16eff> that can be claimed from the Achievements tab.

Rewards are per-player and persist across sessions. Claimed rewards cannot be claimed again.

---

## <#ff6600>How It Works</#ff6600>

- Progress is tracked automatically as you play
- Multi-step achievements show a progress bar (e.g., "Kill 50 Ghouls: 23/50")
- Completed achievements are marked with a gold border
- Unclaimed rewards pulse to draw attention
- All progress saves to the database (if enabled) or locally
