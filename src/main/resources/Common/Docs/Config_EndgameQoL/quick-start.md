# <gradient data="#55ffff:#d16eff">Quick Start — Player Guide</gradient>

New to Endgame & QoL? This page walks you through the gameplay loop from your first endgame craft to the final boss.

---

## <#FFD700>Step 1 — Craft the Endgame Workbench</#FFD700>

The <#55ffff>Endgame Workbench</#55ffff> is your gateway to all endgame content. Craft it to unlock recipes for dungeon keys, weapons, armor, and challenge items.

---

## <#aaaaaa>Step 2 — Gear Up: Adamantite</#aaaaaa>

Adamantite is the bridge between vanilla and endgame. Craft an <#aaaaaa>Adamantite Longsword</#aaaaaa> or <#aaaaaa>Adamantite Staff</#aaaaaa> at the Weapon Bench. Grab Adamantite Armor from the Armor Bench.

**Optional** — Try <#aaaaaa>Warden's Trial Tier I</#aaaaaa> to test your gear. Craft the challenge item at the Endgame Bench Tier 1.

---

## <#55ffff>Step 3 — Frozen Dungeon</#55ffff>

Craft a <#55ffff>Frozen Dungeon Key</#55ffff> (10 Adamantite Bars, 5 Mithril Bars, 30 Fire Essence) at the Endgame Bench Tier 2. Use it on an <#aaaaaa>Ancient Portal</#aaaaaa> to enter.

**Inside the dungeon:**
- Fight through ice enemies (Rats, Toads, Ferans, Golems, Yetis)
- Collect <#55ffff>Flocons</#55ffff> (dungeon currency) to trade with <#55ffff>Korvyn</#55ffff> the hidden merchant
- Mine <#55ffff>Mithril Snow Ore</#55ffff> for Mithril Bars
- Loot chests in 6 progressive zones
- Face <#55ffff>Dragon Frost</#55ffff> (1400 HP, 3-phase fight) at the deepest level

**Key drops** — Dragon Heart, Mithril Bars (4-5), Storm Hide, Ice Essence, and a 2% chance for the <#55ffff>Frostbite Blade</#55ffff>.

---

## <#55ffff>Step 4 — Mithril Tier</#55ffff>

With Mithril Bars from the dungeon, craft your first Mithril weapons and armor at the Endgame Bench Tier 3. Mithril gear provides:
- Mana and Signature Energy bonuses
- +0.5/sec mana regen per armor piece
- Daggers deal 1.5x backstab damage

! Check <#55ffff>Vorthak</#55ffff> in the Forgotten Temple — he trades consumables and Mithril Bars for Adamantite.

---

## <#55ff55>Step 5 — Swamp Dungeon</#55ff55>

Craft a <#55ff55>Swamp Dungeon Key</#55ff55> at the Endgame Bench Tier 3 and enter the Swamp Dungeon.

**Inside the dungeon:**
- Fight Fen Stalkers, Groobles, Spirit Roots, and the <#ff8800>Bramble Elite</#ff8800> mini-boss (550 HP)
- Earn <#55ffff>Swamp Currency</#55ffff> to trade with <#55ffff>Morghul</#55ffff> (sells Onyxium gear and <#55ff55>Hedera Daggers</#55ff55>)
- Collect key ingredients: Swamp Ingot, Swamp Gem, Crocodile Scale, Infused Rope, Hedera's Bramble
- Craft the <#FFD700>Hedera Key</#FFD700> at the Hedera Autel to face <#55ff55>Hedera</#55ff55> (1800 HP, poison + root mechanics)

**Tip** — <#55ffff>Frostbite Blade</#55ffff> deals 30% bonus damage to Hedera!

---

## <#ff6600>Step 6 — Onyxium Tier</#ff6600>

Onyxium Bars drop from Hedera and Golem Void, or trade from Morghul. Craft Onyxium weapons and armor for:
- Special weapon effects (stamina drain, slow, AOE pull)
- ~61% Physical resist (full armor set)
- Passive HP regen after 15s without damage

---

## <#d16eff>Step 7 — The Golem Void</#d16eff>

Craft a <#d16eff>Shard of the Void</#d16eff> (5 Onyxium Bars, 50 Void Essence) at the Endgame Bench Tier 4. This is the hardest fight in the mod.

**<#d16eff>Golem Void</#d16eff>** — 3500 HP, 3 phases, 13 unique attacks, spawns Eye Void minions. Watch for <#d16eff>void runic circles</#d16eff> on the ground — they telegraph heavy attacks.

**Key drops** — Onyxium Bars (6-10), Prisma Bars (3-7), Emerald Gems, Shard of the Radiant Light.

---

## <#d16eff>Step 8 — Prisma Legendaries</#d16eff>

The ultimate tier. Craft Prisma gear at the Endgame Bench Tier 5 using Prisma Bars, Prismic Leather, and Emerald Gems.

**<#d16eff>Prisma Sword</#d16eff>** — Prismatic Beam (Ability3, 80 Mana, 20-block projectile with AOE explosion) + Prismatic Judgment signature (100 SE, 10-block ground slam, 250 dmg + knockup + slow)

**<#d16eff>Prisma Daggers</#d16eff>** — Prisma Dash (Ability3, 60 Mana, 10-block line lunge, 80 dmg per hit) + Razor Storm signature (100 SE, 3 AOE bursts, 240 dmg total)

**<#d16eff>Prisma Pickaxe</#d16eff>** — 3x3 area break toggle, best mining tool

**<#d16eff>Prisma Armor</#d16eff>** — ~78% Physical resist, +4.0/sec mana regen, +3.0 HP/sec passive regen

---

## <#ff5555>Side Content</#ff5555>

Don't forget to explore these systems along the way:

**<#FFD700>Bounty System</#FFD700>** — Daily quests with rewards and reputation. Use <#55ffff>/eg journal</#55ffff>.

**<#FFD700>Achievements</#FFD700>** — 24 achievements across 5 categories. Use <#55ffff>/eg journal</#55ffff>.

**<#55ff55>Bestiary</#55ff55>** — Discover all 32 NPCs and earn kill milestone rewards. Use <#55ffff>/eg journal</#55ffff>.

**<#ff5555>Combo Meter</#ff5555>** — Kill streaks grant up to x4 damage + special effects.

**<#ff5555>Warden's Trial</#ff5555>** — 4-tier wave survival challenge with boss fights and rare loot.

**<#d16eff>Trinket Pouch</#d16eff>** — Equip up to 5 accessories for passive bonuses (Frostwalkers, Void Amulet, Blazefist, etc.)
