# <gradient data="#ff6600:#FFD700:#ff6600">Bounty System</gradient>

The Bounty System offers daily quests with tiered rewards, streak bonuses, and a reputation rank progression.

**Command** — Use <#55ffff>/eg bounty</#55ffff> to open the Bounty Board.

---

## <#ff6600>Bounty Types</#ff6600>

**Kill NPC** — Defeat a specific number of target NPCs.

**Kill Any Boss** — Defeat any boss.

**Speed Kill Boss** — Defeat a specific boss within a time limit (e.g., Dragon Frost in under 3 minutes).

**Craft Item** — Craft a specified item at any bench.

**Dungeon Clear** — Complete a dungeon run.

**Damage Dealt** — Deal a total amount of damage.

**Combo Tier** — Reach a combo tier during a kill streak.

**Weekly: Kill Unique Bosses** — Defeat a set number of distinct boss types (repeated kills of the same boss don't count).

---

## <#FFD700>Reward Pools</#FFD700>

Bounties are drawn from 5 difficulty-tiered reward pools. Higher difficulty bounties offer better rewards but are harder to complete.

Each bounty grants <#FFD700>XP</#FFD700> and <#d16eff>reputation points</#d16eff> upon claiming.

---

## <#55ffff>Streak Bonuses</#55ffff>

Completing bounties on consecutive days builds a streak. Streaks grant bonus rewards on top of the base bounty payout.

---

## <#d16eff>Reputation Ranks</#d16eff>

Earn reputation points by completing bounties. Higher ranks unlock bonus reward multipliers.

| Rank | Points | Bonus |
|:-----|-------:|:------|
| <#aaaaaa>Novice</#aaaaaa> | 0 | — |
| <#55ffff>Veteran</#55ffff> | 500 | +10% rewards |
| <#ff6600>Elite</#ff6600> | 1,500 | +20% rewards |
| <#FFD700>Legend</#FFD700> | 3,000 | +30% rewards |

! Your current rank and progress are displayed in the Bounty Board UI with a progress bar and reward previews.

---

## <#aaaaaa>Weekly Challenges</#aaaaaa>

In addition to daily bounties, weekly challenges offer larger goals with premium rewards. These reset every 7 days.
