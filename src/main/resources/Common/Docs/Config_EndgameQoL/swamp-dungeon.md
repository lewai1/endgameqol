# <gradient data="#55ff55:#2a6e2a:#55ff55">Swamp Dungeon</gradient>

The Swamp Dungeon is an expansion to the Hedera encounter area, adding new crafting, enemies, items, and a trader economy.

## <#55ff55>Dungeon Map</#55ff55>

![Swamp Dungeon Map {600x0}](Docs/Images/SwampDungeonMap.png)

---

## <#55ff55>Hedera Autel</#55ff55>

The <#55ff55>Hedera Autel</#55ff55> is a crafting bench found inside the Swamp Dungeon. It is used to craft the <#FFD700>Hedera Key</#FFD700>, which unlocks deeper areas of the dungeon.

### Hedera Key Recipe

<#aaaaaa>Swamp Ingot</#aaaaaa> x1

<#aaaaaa>Swamp Crocodile Scale</#aaaaaa> x1

<#55aaff>Swamp Gem</#55aaff> x1

<#aaaaaa>Infused Rope</#aaaaaa> x1

<#55ff55>Hedera's Bramble</#55ff55> x1

---

## <#ff8800>Bramble Elite</#ff8800>

**HP** — 550 | **Damage** — 90 Physical (Bite) + 70 Physical (Swipe) + Poison T3
**Category** — Elite mini-boss

A massive thorned beast with a 3-attack combo chain. Displays an elite boss bar when engaged.

**Drops** — <#55ff55>Hedera's Bramble</#55ff55> (guaranteed), <#55ffff>Swamp Currency</#55ffff>

---

## <#55ffff>Morghul — Swamp Trader</#55ffff>

<#55ffff>Morghul</#55ffff> is a merchant NPC found inside the Swamp Dungeon. He trades <#55ffff>Swamp Currency</#55ffff> for various items.

### Trades

| You Get | Cost |
|:--------|:-----|
| <#55ff55>Swamp Ingot</#55ff55> | 6 Swamp Currency |
| <#ff6600>Onyxium gear</#ff6600> | Various prices |
| <#55ff55>Hedera Daggers</#55ff55> | 40 Swamp Currency |
| Potions | Various prices |
| <#55ff55>Forest Essence</#55ff55> | Various prices |

! Hedera Daggers are trade-only — there is no crafting recipe.

---

## <#FFD700>New Items</#FFD700>

**<#55ff55>Hedera's Bramble</#55ff55>** — Dropped by Bramble Elite (guaranteed). Used to craft the Hedera Key. Stacks to 100.

**<#FFD700>Hedera Key</#FFD700>** — Crafted at Hedera Autel. Unlocks locked dungeon doors. Stacks to 10.

**<#55aaff>Swamp Gem</#55aaff>** — Found in breakable gem nodes inside the dungeon. Stacks to 100.

**Swamp Ingot** — Traded from Morghul or found in chests. Stacks to 100.

**<#aaaaaa>Swamp Crocodile Scale</#aaaaaa>** — Dropped by Swamp Crocodile. Crafting ingredient for Hedera Key. Stacks to 100.

**<#aaaaaa>Infused Rope</#aaaaaa>** — Found in hidden dungeon chests. Crafting ingredient for Hedera Key. Stacks to 100.

**<#55ffff>Swamp Currency</#55ffff>** — Dropped by Bramble Elite, Swamp Crocodile, Fen Stalker, and Hedera.

---

## <#55ff55>Dungeon Enemies</#55ff55>

**<#ff8800>Swamp Crocodile</#ff8800>** — 900 HP elite. Massive crocodilian with devastating ambush strikes. Drops Swamp Crocodile Scale, Bone Fragment, Void Essence, Onyxium Bar, and Swamp Currency.

**<#ff8800>Bramble Elite</#ff8800>** — 550 HP elite mini-boss (see above).

**<#55ff55>Fen Stalker</#55ff55>** — 200 HP swift amphibian predator with powerful jaws.

**<#55ff55>Grooble</#55ff55>** — 250 HP burrowing beast with stone-covered hide.

**<#55aaff>Spirit Root</#55aaff>** — 200 HP nature spirit that entangles prey with spectral roots.

---

## <#aaaaaa>Block Protection</#aaaaaa>

Blocks inside the Swamp Dungeon are unbreakable and unplaceable by default, except for <#55aaff>Swamp Gem</#55aaff> nodes which can still be broken. This is controlled by <#aaaaaa>EnableDungeonBlockProtection</#aaaaaa> in <#55ffff>/egconfig</#55ffff> (enabled by default).

---

## <#55ffff>Chest Loot</#55ffff>

The dungeon features 4 tiers of chest loot with swamp-themed progression:

**Common** — Basic supplies and swamp materials

**Rare** — Mid-tier materials and currency

**Epic** — High-tier crafting ingredients

**Hidden** — Best loot, includes dedicated Infused Rope chest
